Chroma - Palette Generator

A powerful yet simple Mac OS app to manage your color palettes.

  • Create palettes from stunning templates or import them directly from your Xcode projects .xcassets files.
  • Easily visualize & modify palettes variants (Any, Default, Dark & High Contrast Versions).
  • Export palettes as .xcassets to use them directly on your projects.
  • Create new shiny colors variations using colors you already love ♥️.
  • Seamlessly add generated colors to palettes.
