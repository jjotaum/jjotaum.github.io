## **Chroma: Palette Manager**
#### Privacy Policy
<sub>Last updated: April 5, 2026</sub>

- **Chroma** does not collect, track, or analyze any personal data. No analytics or tracking tools are used.

- Your information stays entirely on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers, whether operated by us or by third parties.

- An internet connection is not required to use the app. However, connectivity is recommended if you choose to enable iCloud syncing, which allows your data to stay up to date across your devices.
