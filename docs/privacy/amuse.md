## **Amuse: Music Player**
#### Privacy Policy
<sub>Last updated: April 5, 2026</sub>

- **Amuse** does not collect, track, or analyze any personal data. No analytics or tracking tools are used.

- Your information stays entirely on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers, whether operated by us or by third parties.

- **Amuse** is designed with a focus on your local (device-synced) music library and works fully offline. An internet connection is only required if you choose to play content from your streaming library.
