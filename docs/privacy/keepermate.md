## **KeeperMate**
#### Privacy Policy
<sub>Last updated: April 5, 2026</sub>

- **KeeperMate** does not collect, track, or analyze any personal data. No analytics or tracking tools are used.
 
- Your information stays entirely on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers, whether operated by us or by third parties.

- An internet connection is required to fetch bookmark metadata, enable iCloud syncing, and to open links, either in your browser or within the app. These requests are performed directly and are not used for tracking or data collection.
